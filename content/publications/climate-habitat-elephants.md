title: 'Climate-driven Habitat Changes and Refugia Dynamics for Asian Elephants in Bhutan under CMIP6 Scenarios'

venue: 'Ecology & Evolution'

Ensemble species distribution modelling across 96 CMIP6 climate scenarios to quantify present and future habitat suitability, refugia extent, and human–elephant conflict risk for Asian elephants (_Elephas maximus_) across Bhutan's national extent.
